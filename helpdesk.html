FAQ


How do I become a tutor on this platform?

Answer: To become a tutor, you need to register on our website and subscribe to the tutor subscription plan. Once subscribed, you can create and list your courses.

What information do I need to provide when registering a course

Answer: When registering as a tutee, you'll need to provide basic information such as your name, email address, and possibly your educational background or preferences.

How do I cancel my subscription as a tutee?

Answer: You can cancel your subscription as a tutee at any time by accessing your account settings and selecting the option to cancel your subscription.

Can tutors cancel their courses after they've been listed?

Answer: Yes, tutors have the flexibility to cancel their courses at any time. However, we encourage tutors to provide advance notice to minimize inconvenience for enrolled tutees.

How are payments processed for course bookings?

Answer: Payments for course bookings are processed securely through our website using integrated payment gateways. You can choose your preferred payment method during the booking process.

How are payments processed for course bookings?

Answer: Payments for course bookings are processed securely through our website using integrated payment gateways. You can choose your preferred payment method during the booking process.

Is there a refund policy for course bookings?

Answer: Our refund policy may vary depending on the circumstances. Generally, refunds are provided if the tutor cancels the course or in certain exceptional cases. Please refer to our terms and conditions for details.

How can I communicate with my tutor before booking a course?

Answer: You can send messages to tutors through our platform to inquire about course details, availability, or any other questions you may have.

Are there any requirements for tutors to list their courses?

Answer: Tutors are required to provide accurate and detailed information about their courses, including course content, schedule, pricing, and any prerequisites or requirements for students.

How can I provide feedback on a course I've completed?

Answer: After completing a course, you'll have the opportunity to provide feedback and ratings for the tutor and the course through our platform. Your feedback helps us maintain the quality of our services and improve the learning experience for future students.

What happens after I book a course?

Answer: After booking a course, you'll receive confirmation details and instructions from the tutor. You can then prepare for the course according to the provided information.
